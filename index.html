Target Audience Segmentation

Prioritised B2B targets and how we solve their specific pain points.

Tier 1: Large Chemical Manufacturers Highest Priority

Profile & Targets

Revenue: €1B+ annually, 10+ facilities
Targets: BASF, DSM-Firmenich, Evonik, Clariant, Cargill
Personas: VP Process Dev, Head of Biotech R&D, Plant Ops
Focus: Germany, NL, Denmark, USA

The Pain Points

  • Legacy batch processes hitting productivity ceilings
  • Inability to commercialise inhibiting products
  • Pressure to meet sustainability targets without huge CAPEX

Our Value Proposition

  • Retrofit capability: Upgrade existing fermenters vs new builds (faster ROI, lower risk)
  • Cost reduction: 20-50% COGS reduction through higher yield
  • Portfolio enablement: Unlock uncommercial molecules

Engagement Strategy

Direct ABM outreach, custom ROI models showing retrofit economics, invitations to BBEPP for live demonstration.

Tier 2: Medium-Sized Specialty Manufacturers High Priority

Profile: Regional bio-based chemical producers, flavour/fragrance specialists (Symrise tier), €100M-€1B revenue.
Pain Points: Need differentiation vs large competitors, limited R&D budget for process innovation.

Our Value Proposition

  • First-mover advantage in their niche with cost-competitive processes
  • Access to FASTMINI for lab-scale validation (lower barrier)
  • Pathway to commercial scale with staged approach

Tier 3: CDMOs / CMOs Strategic Enablers

Profile: Bio Base Europe Pilot Plant, Rentschler, Novasep.
Pain Points: Need to offer differentiated capabilities, clients demand cost-effective scale-up pathways.

Our Value Proposition

  • Expand service portfolio with continuous fermentation capability
  • Attract clients with challenging (inhibiting) products
  • Share revenue model (premium charging)

Tier 4: SynBio Startups Long-term Pipeline

Profile: Precision fermentation (Ginkgo network, Perfect Day tier), Series A-C.
Pain Points: Struggling to achieve cost-competitive production at scale, investors demanding profitability.

Our Value Proposition

  • Overcome strain limitations through process innovation
  • Demonstrate commercial viability to investors

Note: Engage primarily through Ginkgo network referrals due to startup budget constraints.

Strategic Positioning & Messaging

How we communicate our value and the pre-approved language to use.

The Core Positioning Statement

"For bio-based manufacturers struggling with product inhibition that limits yield and profitability, DAB.bio's FAST platform is a proven continuous fermentation technology that delivers 20-50% cost reduction and up to 10x output increases by enabling in-situ product removal at industrial scale, unlike membrane-based or batch alternatives that compromise on scalability, reliability, or economics."

The 4 Message Pillars

Pillar 1: Proven Industrial Performance

Headline: "FAST Works at Scale: 500L Pilot, 44+ Runs, Peer-Reviewed Results"

  • Operational at BBEPP since 2023
  • 2x output increase in published 2PE study
  • Zero contamination across 44 runs
Pillar 2: Economic Transformation

Headline: "Break the Cost Barrier: 20-50% COGS Reduction"

  • Retrofit economics: upgrade vs new plant (€10M+)
  • Smaller footprint, lower energy costs
  • Faster time-to-market (12-18mo vs 3-5yr)
Pillar 3: Gravity-Based Simplicity

Headline: "No Membranes, No External Loops, No Complexity"

  • Eliminates clogging, fouling, mechanical failure
  • Integrated design (fermentation + extraction)
  • Lower OPEX: no membrane replacement
Pillar 4: Broad Applicability

Headline: "Platform Technology: Chemicals, Flavours, Fuels, Proteins"

  • Proven: butanol, 2PE, terpenes
  • Scalable: lab (2L) to pilot (500L) to commercial (10k+ L)
